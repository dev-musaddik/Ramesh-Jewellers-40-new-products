Our story

A quiet obsession with getting the stone right.

Ramesh Jewellers began in 1998 with a single bench, a loupe and a belief that a diamond should be chosen with the same care as the moment it marks.

Sourced with intent

We buy stones individually, grading to D–F colour and VVS–VS clarity, and turn away far more than we keep. Nothing enters the collection without a certificate we'd stake our name on.

Set to last generations

Every setting is hand-finished in our London workshop — including heritage forms like the mangalsutra, made to designs passed down through families.
